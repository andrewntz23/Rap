The Diachronic Growth of Joey Bada$$

Our Project Goals

This is a digital humanities project aimed at looking at the growth of rapper Joey Bada$$ through poetic, thematic, and linguistic devices. More information will be added as the project progesses. For now, check out the Team Bada$$ bios below!

Team Bada$$
